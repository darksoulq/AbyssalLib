ResourcePack

AbyssalLib comes with two ways of hosting its Resource Packs (Builtin and ResourcePackManager Integration)

Built in Hosting

To enable builtin hosting open /config/abyssallib/config.yml and set the values:

resource_pack: enabled: true host: "your-numerical-ip" port: your-port

RSPM Integration

In case ResourcePackManager is installed AbyssalLib will automatically utilize it for ResourcePack hosting.
